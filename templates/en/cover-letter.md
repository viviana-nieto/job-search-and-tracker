# Cover Letter Template

## Two Variants

### Variant A: Standard (Strategy/PM Roles)

Use for: General PM, Strategy, Analytics roles

```
Hi [Company] team,

I'm excited to apply for [Role]. [Company]'s mission to [specific mission statement] resonates with me, and this role is exactly the kind of work I do best: [what you do best tailored to role].

{{cred_long}}

What I'd bring to [Company]'s [specific area] is a practical, metrics-first approach: break down ambiguous problems, form clear hypotheses, run the analysis or experiments needed to de-risk decisions, and translate insights into roadmap action in partnership with [relevant stakeholders].

Thank you for your consideration. I'd love to discuss how I can help [Company] [specific goal].

{{sign_off_formal}}

{{name}}
{{title}}
M. {{phone}}
E. {{email}}
W. {{website}}
```

### Variant B: Mission-Driven (Personal Story)

Use for: Companies where you have a personal connection to the mission

```
[Date]

Dear [Company] Hiring Team,

I recently [how you found the role]. [Role] is [why it's a strong fit], sitting directly at the intersection of [X and Y] where I have done my best work.

My interest in [Company] is personal. [Personal story connecting you to their mission]. When I see what you have built, [specific thing they do], I feel [emotional connection to their work]. I want to help [their impact] for more people.

{{cred_long}}

[Company-specific paragraph about their stage/needs and what you'd bring]

Warm regards,

{{name}}
{{title}}
M. {{phone}}
E. {{email}}
W. {{website}}
```

## Key Elements to Customize

1. **Opening**: Role name + company mission (research their actual mission)
2. **Self-positioning**: Match your "what I do best" to role requirements
3. **Experience selection**: Choose most relevant from your career arc
4. **Technical mention**: Include if role requires it
5. **Stakeholder list**: Match to role (DS, Eng, Design, Finance, C-Suite, etc.)
6. **Closing goal**: Specific to company (accelerate growth, scale platform, etc.)

## Writing Rules

- Follow rules from config/writing-style.json
- Quantify everything with real numbers from your experience
- Show genuine enthusiasm grounded in specifics
- Keep to one page (4-5 paragraphs max)

## Variables

- `[Company]` - Company name
- `[Role]` - Full role title
- `[Date]` - Current date (for formal letters)
- `[specific mission statement]` - Research and insert
- `[specific area]` - Team or product area
- `[relevant stakeholders]` - Teams you'd work with
- `[specific goal]` - Company objective you'd help with
