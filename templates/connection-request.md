# LinkedIn Connection Request Template

## Constraints
- **Maximum 300 characters** (LinkedIn limit)
- Must include: Hook, credibility, soft CTA
- Sign off with `{{sign_off_linkedin}}`

## Structure

```
[Warm opener], [specific context about them/role]. [Brief credibility]. [Soft CTA]! {{sign_off_linkedin}}
```

## Pattern Elements

1. **Warm opener**: "Hi [Name]," or "Hi [Name], hope your week is off to a great start."
2. **Specific context**: Role applied for, their content, mutual interest, shared background
3. **Brief credibility**: One punchy line ({{cred_short}})
4. **Value hook**: What you bring
5. **Soft CTA**: "Would love to connect!" or "Love to learn more about [X]!"
6. **Sign-off**: `{{sign_off_linkedin}}`

## Example Patterns

### Applied for Role
```
Hi [Name], hope your week is off to a great start. I applied for the [Role] role at [Company] and came across your profile. I'm a {{cred_short}} and I'd love to connect with someone who knows this space as well as you do! {{sign_off_linkedin}}
```

### Saw Their Content
```
Hi [Name], I saw your post about [topic] and loved your thinking. {{cred_short}}. Would love to connect! {{sign_off_linkedin}}
```

### Mutual Connection
```
Hi [Name], [Mutual] mentioned you're doing great work at [Company]. I'm exploring [target role] roles and would love to connect. {{cred_short}}. {{sign_off_linkedin}}
```

### Same Company Target
```
Hi [Name], I'm exploring opportunities at [Company] and saw you're on the [Team] team. I'd love to learn more about the culture there. {{cred_short}}. {{sign_off_linkedin}}
```

## Variables to Fill

- `[Name]` - First name of connection
- `[Company]` - Target company
- `[Role]` - Specific role title
- `[Mutual]` - Mutual connection name (if applicable)
- `[Team]` - Their team at company

## Character Count Check

Before finalizing, verify the message is under 300 characters. If over, cut:
1. "hope your week is off to a great start" (use just "Hi [Name],")
2. Reduce credibility to shortest form
3. Simplify CTA
